New ways to look dapper through the season

Self care is the best care

In a friendly tone: I’m sure there are some extroverts out there who turn inward to recharge. You can take good care of your skin and hair this season by ensuring you wear sunscreen to protect your skin and shade up. The key to feeling happy and energetic is to ensure you drink water, ensure you take a day of rest to care for your skin and hair. We all hate breakouts especially when we need to look our best the next day. Fortunately, we have found an emergency overnight solution. After cleansing your skin with the cleanser, apply the Moisturizing & Corrective Mask, continuing with the Lotion Spot Treatment. Cosmetics makers have always sold “hope in a jar”—creams and potions that promise youth, beauty, sex appeal, and even love for the women who use them. Over the last few years, the marketers at Giveo have added some new-and-improved enticements. They’re now promising self-esteem and cultural transformation. There is a press release, is “a global effort that is intended to serve as a starting point for societal change and act as a catalyst for widening the definition and discussion of beauty.” Along with its thigh-firming creams, self-tanners, and hair conditioners, Giveo is peddling the crowd-pleasing notions that beauty is a media creation, that recognizing plural forms of beauty is the same as declaring every woman beautiful, and that self-esteem means ignoring imperfections.
